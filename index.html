Introduction
Greetings! I'm a Ph.D. student at Language Technologies Institute under the supervision of Prof. Alexander Hauptmann.
I obtained my Master's degree from the same institute, major in Artificial Intelligence and Innovation.
My research interests are self-supervised/weakly-supervised learning, video analytics, and 3D vision.
